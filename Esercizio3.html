Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized inions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups(500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups(350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra vergin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scarpe rind off with paring knife if desidered, though it's not necessary
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

    Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix toghether all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
